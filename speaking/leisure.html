Speaking Part 1: Leisure Time

What is your favourite leisure activity?

When I have free time, I love going to a spa to take care of my body. I usually go on my days off. I enjoy it because it helps me relax, reduce stress, and it makes me feel very comfortable.

What did you enjoy doing in your free time as a child?

When I was a child, I always enjoyed going to amusement parks with my parents. We usually went there about once a week on the weekend, and we always had a really good time together.

Do you prefer to spend your free time with other people or alone?

I prefer to spend my free time alone. Because I meet a lot of people every day at work, I feel I need some time for myself. I feel great when I can have some "me time" to relax.

What is a common leisure activity in your country?

A common leisure activity in my country is singing karaoke. Almost every home has a karaoke system, so we can sing whenever we want. It makes life more lively and also helps us relax and reduce stress.

Do most people in your country get two days off a week?

Yes, most people get two days off a week, usually Saturday and Sunday. However, a lot of people have to work on weekends, especially those in the service sector, like in spas, hair salons, or restaurants.

Do you think leisure time is important?

Yes, I do. I think leisure time is very important because we need to rest and have a good work-life balance. It helps us recharge so we can start the next day full of energy. Without leisure time, we would feel very tired and stressed.
