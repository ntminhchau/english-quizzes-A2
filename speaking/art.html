Speaking Part 1: Art

Are you good at art?

Well, I think I’m a bit good at art. I used to be a makeup artist so I was good at drawing and mixing colors. I really enjoyed it when I saw people become more beautiful with my makeup.

Did you learn art at school when you were a child?

Yes, I did. When I was a child, I studied art twice a week at school. My teacher taught me how to draw animals, flowers and houses. I really enjoyed those lessons because drawing made me feel creative.

Do you ever draw or paint pictures now?

Yes, I often paint pictures with my children when I have free time. Sometimes we often go to the bookstore and enjoy painting little statues together there. It’s very fun and helps me have quality time with my children.

What kind of art do you like?

I like landscape art because it helps me relax. When I look at paintings of beaches, rivers or mountains, I feel very calm and peaceful.

Is art popular in your country?

Yes, it’s very popular in my country because it provides entertainment for both children and adults. Most public facilities have an art activity area such as shopping malls, bookstores or parks. We can paint statues, draw pictures or make crafts there.

When was the last time you went to an art gallery or exhibition?

To be honest, I can’t remember. Maybe it was about 10 years ago, because after I had my children, I didn’t have time to go to art galleries.

What kind of pictures do you like having in your home?

I like having pictures of beautiful scenery such as beaches, rivers or mountains in my living room because they make me feel peaceful when I look at them. They also make my house more beautiful.
