Speaking Part 1: Internet

How often do you go online?

I go online every day, mostly in the evening after work. I usually spend about two hours online, but on weekends, I go online more to watch videos or chat with my friends.

What do you use the internet for?

I usually use the internet to check news, watch videos, and chat with my friends. Sometimes I also use it for work or to learn English. It’s very useful because it helps me find information quickly.

How do you get online?

I usually get online with my smartphone or laptop using Wi-Fi at home. When I’m outside, I sometimes use mobile data. It’s very convenient because I can connect almost anywhere.

Do you have your own computer?

No, I don’t have a computer at the moment. I have to borrow my nephew’s laptop when I need to study English. However, it’s a bit inconvenient, so I think I’ll buy my own computer in the future.

What's your favourite website?

I think my favourite website is Facebook. I always use it to connect with my friends. Besides, I also read the latest news there, and sometimes I post my pictures to share with my friends and family.

Do you think children should be allowed unsupervised access to the internet?

No, I don’t think so because there are many different types of information on the internet. Some of it is very useful, but some can be harmful for children. That’s why I think children should be supervised by parents when using the internet.
