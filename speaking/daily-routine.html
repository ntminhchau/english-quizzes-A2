Speaking Part 1: Daily Routine

When do you usually get up in the morning?

I usually get up at 7 AM, I sit on my bed and take a deep breath to start my day. On the weekend, I get up later, around 10 o’clock, because I don’t have work.

What is your typical daily routine?

Everyday, I get up at 7 AM and then I take a shower. After that, I go out to have breakfast and go to work. I finish work at around 5 PM. After work, if I have time, I’ll go shopping and then prepare dinner. After dinner, I relax a little with my family. Before I go to bed, I kiss my children good night.

Is your routine the same on weekdays and weekends?

It’s almost the same because every day I go to work. However, it’s a bit different at the weekend because I can get up late, and I clean my house and go shopping or hang out with my family.

Do you ever change your routine?

Yes, I change it sometimes. On special days like public holidays, I do different things like going to church or visiting my parents.

Is your routine now different from when you were a child?

It's absolutely different. When I was a child, I went to school and I couldn’t go shopping by myself. Now I go to work and I have to prepare everything for my family.

Do you think it's important to have a routine?

Yes, I think so. It helps me not to forget what to do every day and keep me organized. I feel more relaxed with a routine. Without a routine, I feel lost.
