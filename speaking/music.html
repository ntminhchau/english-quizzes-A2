Speaking Part 1: Music

Do you like music?

Yes, I love music. When I have free time, I usually listen to music to relax and reduce stress. Besides, music also calms my soul and helps me sleep well.

What's your favourite type of music?

It depends. When I want to relax, I usually listen to bolero and meditation music. On the other hand, when I do exercise, I prefer dance music to give me more energy.

Do you have a favourite song you like listening to?

Yes, my favourite song is called “Hold me for a while”. I like it because the lyrics are quite similar to my own love story. Whenever I listen to it, it reminds me of my first love and brings back a lot of memories.

Can you sing?

Yes, I can sing very well. I started singing when I was a teenager. I usually sing when I’m doing housework or taking a shower. At the weekend, I often sing karaoke with my family.

How important is singing in your culture?

Singing is very important in my culture. People often sing at parties, weddings, festivals and so on. I think Vietnamese people really love singing and that’s why karaoke is so popular here. We can sing anytime anywhere.

Did you learn music at school?

Yes, when I was a child, I studied music twice a week at school. My teacher taught me how to sing simple children’s songs. I really enjoyed those lessons. My favourite song was one about mothers.

If you could learn a musical instrument, what would it be?

If I could learn a musical instrument, I would choose the piano because it sounds very beautiful. The shape of the piano is also very nice and I think I would feel comfortable when I play it.

Do you think music is important?

Yes, I think music is very important because it helps people relax and reduce stress. It’s also fun listening to music together and it’s a great way to connect with other people. Without music, I think life would be very quiet and boring.
