Speaking Part 1: Photographs

What type of photos do you like taking?

I like taking selfies because I can always look beautiful in them. It makes me happy and feel confident whenever I look at them. Sometimes I also enjoy taking photos of landscapes such as flowers, fields or rivers.

What do you do with photos you take?

I usually store my photos on my phone. When I have free time, I often look at them again to bring back good memories. Sometimes I post my photos on Facebook to share with my family and friends.

When you visit other places, do you take photos or buy postcards?

I prefer to take photos when I visit other places because it’s more personal. I can take photos of whatever I like and I also enjoy editing my photos afterwards to make them look more beautiful.

Do you like people taking photos of you?

Yes, when I can’t take photos of myself, I often ask somebody to help me. Sometimes I enjoy taking photos with my family or friends to keep the good memories with them.
