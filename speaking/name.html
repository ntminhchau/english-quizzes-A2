Speaking Part 1: Name

What's your name?

My name is Thuy Phuong. You can call me Phuong. It's spelled P-H-U-O-N-G. Most people say it's difficult to pronounce. It's a common name in my country.

How did your parents choose your name(s)?

My parents chose it because my name means true direction and my mom wanted me to have a right direction in life.

Does your name have any special meaning?

Yes, my name means true direction. My parents chose it because my mom wanted me to have a right direction in life.

Is your name common or unusual in your country?

It's a very common name in my country. It can be used for both boys and girls. People often say it's a nice name.

If you could change your name, would you? [Why/Why not?]

Yes, I would because the name Phuong in Vietnamese means direction so I don't know which direction to go. If I could change my name, I would change it to Rowena, because it means famous and happy.
