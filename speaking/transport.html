Speaking Part 1: Transport

How did you get here today?

I came here by car. It took me 20 minutes because the traffic was light this morning. I left home quite early because I was afraid I would be late.

What is your favourite mode of transport?

My favourite mode of transport is the motorbike because it’s easy to ride. Besides, it’s small, so it’s very convenient to get around the city.

Do you ever use public transport?

Yes, I usually use public transport such as buses, trains, and the underground. I use the bus most often because the bus stop is near my house. I take the bus once a week to go shopping at the weekend.

Do you like the transport system in your country?

Yes, I do, I like the transport system in my country because it’s very convenient. It provides transport services everywhere. There are a lot of buses, coaches, and trains available in most cities.

What is the difference between taking a bus and taking a train?

I think buses are easy to catch because they are out on the street. Moreover, they have more stops, so you don’t have to walk very far. Therefore, buses are more convenient for short distances in the city. On the other hand, trains are faster, so it’s more convenient when you want to travel to places outside the city.
