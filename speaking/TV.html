Speaking Part 1: TV

Do you often watch TV?

Yes, I often watch TV when I have free time or when I want to relax after dinner with my family. I especially enjoy watching horror movies on TV on the weekends.

Where do you usually watch TV programmes/shows?

I usually watch TV programmes in the living room at home because it’s comfortable. I often watch TV with my family after we finish dinner to relax together.

What sorts of things do you watch on TV?

I usually watch news on TV because I want to get up-to-date information. Moreover, I enjoy fashion programmes because I love fashion. Sometimes I also like watching music shows to relax.

What is your favourite TV program?

My favourite TV program is Fashion TV because I love fashion and I want to learn new designs and get fresh ideas. I hope to become a fashion designer one day. That is my dream.

Are there any programmes/shows you don't like watching?

I think I don’t like watching political programs because I find them very boring and not relaxing at all. People usually talk too much in them and I don’t have time to listen.

Will you watch more or fewer TV programmes/shows in the future?

I think I will watch fewer TV programmes in the future, because nowadays I use the Internet to watch what I want. There are many interesting things online and it’s also more convenient.

Do you ever watch foreign programs or films?

Yes, of course. I really like watching Animal Planet, especially shows about helping animals. Moreover, I love American action and horror films because they look very realistic and make me feel very excited and satisfied.

What did you watch on TV when you were a child?

When I was a child, I usually watched cartoons and fairy tales on TV. I especially enjoyed Tom and Jerry and Snow White. They were very fun and I was always happy when I watched them.

Do you think children should watch TV?

Yes, I do. I think children should watch TV because it teaches them many life skills and help them learn right from wrong. Besides, parents can control what they watch on TV. But they shouldn’t watch too much because it’s not good for their eyes.
