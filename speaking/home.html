Speaking Part 1: Home

Where is your home?

My home is in Ho Chi Minh City. It's located on Nguyen Van Troi street in Phu Nhuan District. It's near a big market. I've lived there for over 40 years. It's a crowded area.

Do you live in a house or a flat?

I live in a house. My house has 2 floors and 4 bedrooms. It has two colors, white and grey. I really love my house because I feel comfortable when I come back home from work.

Who do you live with?

I live with my parents, my husband and my two children. I'm always happy when I'm with them. The children often talk a lot during breakfast and they also make my parents happy.

Are there many rooms in your home?

Yes, there are 7 rooms in my home. We have 4 bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen, and a study. My room is on the second floor and it's quite big. It has a nice balcony.

What is your favourite room?

My favorite room is the living room. It has a big sofa, a karaoke system, a TV and some yellow lights. I like it because it's very cozy and comfortable, and I can relax in this room when I have some pressure in life.

How are the walls decorated?

The walls are painted yellow. There are a lot of pictures on the walls, and some yellow lights above the pictures. There are also some shelves where I put some gifts and small flower vases. It makes the room feel warm and beautiful. I decorated the walls myself because I love decorating my house.

What would you change about your home?

I would change the kitchen because it's a bit small and old. I want to make it bigger and have more shelves where I can put everything because I love cooking and baking. I also want to have a big table for preparing food in the kitchen.

Do you plan to live there in the future?

Yes, I plan to live there for a long time because my house is very beautiful and cozy. I've had a lot of great memories in my house. It's also quite convenient because it's close to most facilities. I always feel happy whenever I come back home from work.

What facilities are there near your home?

There are many facilities near my home like a convenience store, a supermarket, a cinema, a karaoke bar, and many coffee shops. There is also a bank, a spa, a salon,.. everything is near my home. It's very convenient.

What is your neighbourhood like?

My neighbourhood is very lively because there are many shops nearby. The people here are very friendly and we often help each other. Sometimes we even have parties together. I really like my neighbourhood.
