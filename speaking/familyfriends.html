Speaking Part 1: Family & Friends

Do you spend much time with your family?

Yes, I spend a lot of time with them on the weekend. I often make many dishes and relax with my family. We usually go out to eat and drink together. We also go for a walk in the park.

Who are you closest to in your family?

I’m closest to my mother. I always tell her everything and whenever I’m happy or sad I always come to her. She always listens and helps me. I feel happy when I’m with her.

Do you prefer spending time with your family or friends?

I prefer spending time with my family because I feel comfortable with them. However, I feel relaxed when I meet my friends, so I think both are important but family is still my priority.

How often do you go out with friends?

I think I only go out with my friends when I have free time, so maybe once a week. I usually go to a café with them. Sometimes we organize a party together.

Tell me about your best friend at school.

My best friend at school was Ngọc. we were neighbours and we went to school together. We often talked with each other, hung out or even exercised together in the park. She was very smart and friendly. We’re still in touch with each other now.

Are you still friends with people from your childhood?

Yes, I’m still friends with some of my childhood friends. We keep in touch online. Sometimes we meet at a café to catch up. I feel very comfortable gossiping with my old friends.

Is family important in your country?

Yes, family is very important in my country because it is Vietnamese tradition. Children usually live with parents until they get married. Moreover, when we have children, our parents help us take care of our children. We also always gather on Tet holiday.

Which is more important to you, friends or family?

For me, my family is more important. I feel more comfortable with them. We do many things together and can share about everything. Of course my friends are also important but my family is still my priority.
