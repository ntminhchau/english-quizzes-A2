Speaking Part 1: Shopping

Do you like shopping?

Yes, I like shopping very much because it makes me feel comfortable and relaxed in my life. Moreover, I can buy things that are essential for me and it can also help me get some exercise.

What's your favourite shop?

My favourite shop is the local supermarket because there are a lot of goods to choose from, and it’s very convenient - I can buy all the things I need in one place. Besides, they often have big discounts so I can buy things at a good price.

Do you prefer shopping alone or with others?

It depends on who I go with. If the person has the same taste as me, I prefer going shopping together. Otherwise, I’d rather go alone because I can take my time and choose what I want.
