Speaking Part 1: Shopping

Do you like shopping?

Yes, I like shopping very much because it makes me feel comfortable and relaxed in my life. Moreover, I can buy things that are essential for me and it can also help me get some exercise.

What's your favourite shop?

My favourite shop is the local supermarket because there are a lot of goods to choose from, and it’s very convenient - I can buy all the things I need in one place. Besides, they often have big discounts so I can buy things at a good price.

Do you prefer shopping alone or with others?

It depends on who I go with. If the person has the same taste as me, I prefer going shopping together. Otherwise, I’d rather go alone because I can take my time and choose what I want.

Have you ever bought anything online?

Yes, of course. I usually buy clothes, candies and cosmetics online. When I have free time, I always enjoy checking out products on my phone. It’s so convenient nowadays - I can stay at home and still get everything I need.

How often do you buy things online?

Maybe once a week. When I’m busy or can’t go out, I will buy things online. It’s convenient but because I can only see the products in pictures, I still like going to real stores when I have time.

What was the last thing you bought online?

The last thing I bought online was a dress. While I was surfing Facebook, I came across a Cambodian store with a dress I liked, so I bought it. It was blue and made of linen. It’s comfortable to wear in the summer. It was very nice and I was quite satisfied with it.

Do you ever see things in shops and then buy them online?

Yes, sometimes I see something in shops and then buy them online if it’s cheaper. I usually compare prices before I decide to buy something. This way, I feel very happy because I can get good-quality items at a lower price.

Do you think men and women have different opinions about shopping?

Yes, I think men and women have different opinions about shopping. In my experience, men usually prefer quick shopping, but women often like to take more time to compare items.
